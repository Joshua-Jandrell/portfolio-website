Life-Time

A point and click story

Lifetime is a narrative-driven point and click adventure which chronicles the daily struggle between balancing work commitments and a relationship.

I had the pleasure of designing this game in collabiration with Matipa Marufu. Matipa is responsible for the gorgeous art, amazing animation and brilliant storytelling. Collaborating was super fun: it's something I should do way more often.

The dialog, variables and events were written entirely in Inky. I enjoyed the challenge of integrating this with Unity and building a system that would robust enough to allow for constant edits to the story.
