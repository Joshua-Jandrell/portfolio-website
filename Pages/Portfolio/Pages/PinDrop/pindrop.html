Pin Drop

A three-dentinal block-sliding game

Slide the red pin into the hole at the bottom to complete each level. What's that you say? The box is stuck turns out there's a way around that: just give it a spin.

Control the red ball as it guids the blue ball throght a series of obsticles. But don't ever think your blue friend is dead wieght! Be it rolling through walls, pressing buttons or… something else; that blue ball (and it's grey cousin) will be the keys to your success.

Pin Drop was the first unity prototype that I built in under two weeks. The experience was thoughrourly enjoyable. It may not be very fleshed out, but I think Pindrop is fun and I hop you do too - good luck!
