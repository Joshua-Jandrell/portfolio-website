Pin Drop

A three-dentinal block-sliding game

Slide the red pin into the hole at the bottom to complete each level. What's that you say? The box is stuck turns out there's a way around that: just give it a spin.

Pin Drop was the first unity prototype that I built in under two weeks. The experience was thoughrourly enjoyable.

It may not be very fleshed out, but I think Pindrop is fun and feels smooth to play. I hope you do too.
