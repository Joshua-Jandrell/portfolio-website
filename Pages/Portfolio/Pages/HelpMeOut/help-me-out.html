Help Me Out

An abstract puzzle game about support

Desinged to adress the theme of support in an abstract context, Help Me Out will test both your puzzle solving skills and reflexes as you attempt to navigate a duo of coloured balls through a myriad of mazes and obstacles.

Control the red ball as it guids the blue ball throght a series of obsticles. But don't ever think your blue friend is dead wieght! Be it rolling through walls, pressing buttons or… something else; that blue ball (and it's grey cousin) will be the keys to your success.
