Thinking About the next section 13 April 2022 A brief look at the layout of the About section. this section will be directly below the home section. If a visitor keeps moving down the site they will get there next.

Why put the About section next?

I don't know how long a visitor will scroll so it's important I get to the point. Once have show them a bit of what I do (in the home navigation section) I should tell them who I am. The point of the site is to market myself so I should get to the product (me) as soon as possible.

This part should be short an to the point so that the visitor feels it will not take too long to read.

It will make extensive use of collapsible inline text so that if the viewer wants to read more they can, without the section feeling too long or wordy. This also allows vistor to interact with the site, deliveing a more engaging and intresting experance.

I don't want to come off as pretentiously formal so this will be a bit tongue in cheek. As much as I want to seem qualified, I also want to sound interesting.

How will this section be structured?



  • Text offset to the right to balance the left-aligned heading. This will make the section layout appear more interesting and less text-heavy. I assume that this will lead the viewer eyes downwards diagonally. Their eyes will then move from the title to the profile picture and then to the text. They will then move back and read the contact information.
  • Profile pic is in the top left so that the introduction feels personal and the visitor can put a face to my name.
  • Contact icons (for social media etc) will appear at the top left. In this position they are easily visible but do not feel like obnoxious clutter. These icons encourage the visitor to reach out to me (which is exactly what I want them to do).
