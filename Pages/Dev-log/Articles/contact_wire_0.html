Contact section structure


Clickable icons makes it quick and easy for visitors to contact me. They can do it with a single mouse click rather than having to open up their mail app and paste in an address. The structure is simple and to the point - contacting me should be easy and effortless so I'd like to avoid clutter.

WhatsApp and telegram icons will allow visitors to send me messages. I will not display phone number because I want people to email or message me, rather than calling.

I'll pop in a link to my git-hub (even though most of my repos are private at this stage). This is where most of my game code is kept so it makes sent to give curios visitors a chance to take a look.

I'm planning to make an itch account some time soon, so I'll set up a link from that in when its ready.
