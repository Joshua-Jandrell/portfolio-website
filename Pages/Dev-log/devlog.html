What exactly is UX?

 UX stands for user experance. So it is the experance of the user.
Nooooo, really!? I know. Very insigtful.
It's not easy to pinpoint what UX is becuase it is so broad. The general concept of good UX is about making an
artificat (or object; entity; product; thing; group of things; jar of pickels; website; race car; hotel; you get the idea)
feel good to use. How does the user think about the artificat? What thoughs and emotions does it invoke in them? What does it make them want to do? (For example what does this site make you want to do right now?)

To avoid rambling here I should probably white a blognothing yet I fear on this at some point.

Back to the Drawing Baord

30-03-2022 Reflecting on what my website it and deciding what it should be.

What do i have so far...

Not much if I'm honest. Up until now I have had a grand vision for my website: Each scene would be an interactive landscape with conten placed on the backdrop of a beuitiful sky. This would give visitors the experance of exploring a virtual world and a maze them with sutnning imegry. The sky has connotations of freadom and expenasion. I wanted the home page to be a sunrise to invoke the idea of new begginings.

This sounds great! What's the porblem?

This was one of the first ideas I had. It wasn't unthoughtful; however it was hardly the result of maticlous consideration. I love the sky - it looks great. But does it really speak to what I am trying to do? Anyne can go crazy with stock photos of sunrises, but few do. I'm not a photogroper. I'm not a travel agencey. Using the sky as a motif for this website is like using sports cars to advertise fast food. There is some sort of connection, but honestly it's a strach. Interactive landscapes my be fun at first, but they are not the content of my sight. This feature would hurt the perfomace of the website and offer more destraction than excitment.

I see. Coudn't you just dail things down. Why restart?

I could. But restarting will allow be to build a better site. When I began working on the site, I new nothing about web-dev, html, css or javascript. (I still no very little now - but I'm getting there). Th first attempt got messy and didn't really work out how I had planned. I can use that experance to be better this time. This is a learning experance and the best way to learn is to notice mistakes and imporve.

So what will you be doing differently?

  • Keep it simple I was very focused on making my last website intresting for the sake of being intresting (and to show of skills I didn't have but planed to get). There will be a time to add some flare. But for now it;s about baby steps. A slick and porfessional website will probabay make better first impression than an over-complicated and cumbersome one. Website-juice is like game juice-delcious (and fruit juice): delicious in moderation but unhealthy in exccess.
  • Pay more attention to html markup This one is simple. I tried last time but it got confusing. I used mark-up for the sake of it, rather than to describe my site. I have a better idea of what to do now.
  • Design with intent I need a clear vision for what my site is. Then I can desingn a site that can achieve this. I was finding reasons to justfiy what I wanted to do when I should have doing things to deliver the experance wanted to create.
  • Work on the site daily I was not consistent when working on my site. I would often do a single weekly binge session and then get disharented that things were not coming together as I had invisiaged. This is bad bad and bad practicing. The best way to learn and get good at something is short but consitent engangment rather than long sporadic sections. Doing a bit of dev work each day won't nly be good for me to practive, it'll also ensure that the site keeps growing. Furthermore, daily engament will leead to more engaged and throughful desing beacuse of more frequent engamnet. Putting of dev-work just means more time and stress later down the line.
  • Take baby steps One of the reasons I haven't been devloping daily is becuase I find my site ugly and baording. I want to make the finished prodcut. I wan to do cool and inreting things for it. I want to try out so many ideas, but I'm not there yet. I am ecited to bring my website to life with JavaScript, but some of my pages don't even have proper html. I've been trying to install a home enterainment system in a house that doesn't exist. I need to have the dicepline to focus on the task at hand. I must build the scafold and the foundation first, then strat paining and move in. This takes planing and self dicepline - both of which have been lacking.
  • Think less, do more Look at me now. Waffeling on and talking in circles. I have an tendancy to do this. I have a tendacy to overthing. I have a tendacy to make 100 plans and act on none of them. I kept telling myself I would start 'working promperly' when I knew what I was doing. But I won't know what I'm doing unless I learn. And I won't learn unless I start.
    • JUST START ALREADY! This list is whishy-washy. Nobody cares what you did - they care what you've done. so start.
    • Why are you still here!? And why are you talking to yourself?
